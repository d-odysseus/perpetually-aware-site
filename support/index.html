How to use it

  • Launch the app and wait a moment for the title to settle.
  • Touch the dark — tap anywhere — to begin, and again to move to the next statement.
  • Tap near the left edge to step back.
  • The control at the bottom-right toggles an optional ambient sound. It begins muted.
  • There are ten statements, followed by a quiet coda. There is nothing to win or unlock.

Reduce Motion

If you turn on Reduce Motion in iOS Settings, the piece honors it and stills its animation.

Sound

The soundscape is optional and off by default. If you don’t hear it after turning it on, check that your device isn’t muted and the volume is up. The sound pauses when the app is in the background and resumes when you return.

Privacy

The app collects no data and makes no network connections. See the Privacy Policy.
