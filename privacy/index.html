Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

Perpetually Aware is a self-contained interactive art piece. It is built to ask nothing of you, and that includes your data.

What we collect

Nothing. Perpetually Aware does not collect, store, transmit, or share any information about you or your device. There is no account, no sign-in, and no profile.

Network

The app makes no network connections. It runs entirely on your device. Its words, font, soundscape, and visuals are bundled inside the app itself; it never reaches out to a server, and it has no way to send anything off your device.

Tracking and analytics

There is none. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting, and no third-party tracking of any kind. It does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because it does not track you across apps or websites.

Permissions

The app requests no permissions. It does not access your microphone, camera, location, contacts, photos, or local network. The ambient sound is audio output only — the app never listens.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Third-party components

The app uses one open-source software component, flutter_soloud, solely to play its bundled ambient soundscape on your device. It performs no networking and collects no data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new “last updated” date. Because the app collects no data, any change will concern wording rather than practice.
